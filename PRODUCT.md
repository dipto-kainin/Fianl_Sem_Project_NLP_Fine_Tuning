# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Researchers, engineers, and technical users who manage document-based AI workflows. They upload documents (PDFs, DOCX, etc.), trigger knowledge extraction via a Teacher LLM (Gemini), query the knowledge base with RAG, generate training datasets, and fine-tune a lightweight Student model for on-device inference. Primary tasks are document management, semantic querying, and monitoring the distillation pipeline.

## Product Purpose

AI Knowledge Distillation Platform — a pipeline tool that converts proprietary documents into machine-learnable knowledge. It extracts structured understanding via a Teacher LLM, enables fast RAG-based Q&A, and trains a compact Student LLM for on-device, offline inference. Success means users can upload once and query forever — cheaper, faster, private.

## Brand Personality

Precise, capable, understated. The product should feel like a sharp tool built by engineers who respect their users' intelligence. Not flashy, not bland — quiet confidence. Think: "Vercel meets research workbench."

## Anti-references

- Generic purple-glow AI aesthetic (ChatGPT clones, AI startup sameness)
- Beige/warm-paper backgrounds with clay accents (2024 AI product cliché)
- Overly playful dashboards with emoji and confetti
- Dense data-heavy admin panels that feel overwhelming

## Design Principles

1. **The pipeline is the product** — the upload → process → query → train flow should always be legible, not hidden in tabs.
2. **Surface confidence through precision** — exact numbers, real data, no placeholder fuzz.
3. **Motion serves the pipeline state** — animations communicate job status, transitions, and model activity — not decoration.
4. **One question at a time** — the chat/RAG interface is the primary output surface; it deserves full visual focus.
5. **Dark by default** — engineers working with LLMs and document processing work in dark environments; this is not aesthetic choice, it's ergonomic.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion must degrade gracefully — all pipeline status stays visible without animation. Focus states visible. Color is never the sole signal for status.
